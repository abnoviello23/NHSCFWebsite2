Welcome to the National High School Climate Forum

What is our Mission?

Our mission is to bring together sustainability and climate-action focused high schools from around the country to establish an exchange of initative ideas in our respective communities.


Why should your school join us?

There is absolutely no downside to joining us - you don't need to change any of your schools pre-existing climate initiatives. Just tell us about them and be provided with opportunities to showcase them!

Here is what we bring to the table:

  • Forum Platform
  • Speaker Events
  • National Summit

Throughout the year, we aim to provide our member schools with an active forum platform, through which they may gain ideas from the climate initiatives of other schools. Similarly, we provide schools the opportunity to showcase their own ideas on the forum. In order to facilitate this, we will be sending out monthly newsletters, as well as other announcements and reminders.
